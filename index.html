Our Story

Trackside Café is a true labor of love from Bill and Tammy, two lifelong Michiganders who have been serving breakfast with heart for decades. Before opening the doors here in Flushing, they ran the beloved Lennon Café — a small-town favorite known for its warmth, laughter, and plates that always left folks full and happy. When the time came for a new adventure, they brought everything they learned from Lennon right down the tracks to create something even more special.

Today, Trackside blends that same family-first energy with a modern hometown feel. It’s where neighbors meet, coffee never stops pouring, and you’re treated like part of the family from the moment you walk in. Every omelet flipped, pie baked, and pot of soup stirred still carries the spirit of that first café — honest food made with care, served by people who love what they do.
